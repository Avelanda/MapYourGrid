    A curated list of electrical grid maps, datasets, and resources for (almost) every country in the world.

    This dataset is been curated and maintained by the MapYourGrid Initiative. Get started with transmission grid mapping in OpenStreetMap by checking out our Starter Kit Tutorial and our community discord channel.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è These datasets, maps and resources can help you to estimate grid coverage in OpenStreetMap and identify missing lines. Do not copy/paste data directly from these maps into your OpenStreetMap layer, and always keep your data separate from OpenStreetMap data in your editor. Each data point of the transmission network must be set manually and verified with official satellite data provided by the OpenStreetMap community. ‚ö†Ô∏è

    If a resource link is broken, or you find a map with a broken link that you'd like to add to this list, try it on the Internet Archive. This even works for PDFs and other files.
